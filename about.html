Hamari Kahani

Ek chhote clinic se, Patna ke bharose tak.

1999 mein Dr. R.K. Mehta ne sirf 10 beds ke saath Sanjeevani ki shuruaat ki thi — ek simple soch ke saath: "har mareez ko apna parivaar samjho." Aaj, 25 saal baad, hum 180 beds, 60+ specialist doctors aur advanced technology ke saath Patna ke sabse bharosemand hospitals mein se ek hain.

Lekin jo nahi badla, woh hai hamari neeyat — har mareez ko sirf ilaaj nahi, balki dekhbhal aur samman dena.
